# Student Kit

Your AI study partner. Exam prep, study planning, flashcards, note organization, essay help, deadline tracking, and daily productivity — built for students who want better grades with less stress.

---

## What is this?

Being a student means juggling lectures, assignments, exams, group projects, a budget, and somehow having a life. Student Kit turns Claude into a study partner that actually helps — not by doing your work, but by making everything around studying easier.

17 skills that handle the chaos so you can focus on learning.

## What you get

### 📚 Study Tools
- **Study Planner** — Weekly study schedules using spaced repetition. No more cramming.
- **Exam Prep** — Practice questions, summary sheets, and interactive quizzes for any subject.
- **Flashcard Maker** — Generate Q&A flashcards from any topic. Spaced repetition built in.
- **Concept Explainer** — Any concept explained at your level. Multiple styles: simple, visual, practical.
- **Reading Summarizer** — TL;DR for any paper, textbook chapter, or article. Study-ready notes in seconds.
- **Note Organizer** — Dump your messy lecture notes. Get clean, structured, searchable notes back.
- **Essay Helper** — Not a ghostwriter. A tutor that helps you write better papers yourself.

### ⏰ Productivity
- **Daily Briefing** — Quick morning overview: deadlines, assignments, study goals. Under 30 seconds.
- **Deadline Tracker** — All your due dates in one place. Color-coded urgency. Never miss a deadline.
- **Pomodoro Timer** — Focus sessions with goals and reflection. Track your streaks.
- **Weekly Review** — 5-minute Sunday planning. What happened, what's next, what to change.
- **Group Project Manager** — Divide tasks, track contributions, draft team communications. Handle free-riders diplomatically.

### 🎯 Life
- **Quick Capture** — Ideas, notes, questions, tasks — say it and it's saved instantly.
- **Budget Planner** — Simple student budget. "Can I afford this?" answered fast.
- **Motivation Coach** — Stuck? Overwhelmed? Procrastinating? Real help, not motivational posters.
- **Career Prep** — CV, cover letters, interview prep, job search. Ready for life after graduation.
- **Personal Setup** — One-time interview that configures everything around your student life.

## Install

### In Cowork (Claude Desktop App)
1. Go to **Plugins**
2. Add via GitHub: `lukasglarborg/student-kit`
3. Say **"hello"** — the kit sets up your workspace in 2 minutes

### In Claude Code (Terminal)
```bash
claude --plugin-dir path/to/student-kit
```

## How to use it

Just talk like you would to a smart classmate:

| You say | What happens |
|---------|-------------|
| "Plan my study week" | Study schedule based on your deadlines and courses |
| "Quiz me on cell biology" | Practice questions with instant feedback |
| "Make flashcards from today's lecture" | Q&A cards with spaced repetition |
| "Explain quantum entanglement like I'm 5" | Simple explanation with analogies |
| "Summarize this paper" | Key points, arguments, and analysis |
| "I have an essay due Friday on climate policy" | Brainstorm → outline → structured feedback |
| "I can't focus" | One concrete step to get started |
| "Can I afford going out tonight?" | Quick budget check |

**Works in any language.** English, Danish, German, French, Spanish — just answer in your language during setup.

## Academic integrity

Student Kit helps you **learn better**, not cheat. It will never write your assignments. It explains concepts, generates practice material, organizes your notes, and gives feedback on your writing — like a good tutor would.

## Who is this for?

Any student — university, college, high school — who wants to study smarter, stay organized, and stress less. Works for any field: STEM, humanities, business, arts, law, medicine.
